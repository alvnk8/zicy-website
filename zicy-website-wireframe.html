What it does

Four checks, instantly.

AI readiness audit

An instant 0–100 score across seven metrics: answer density, query-fanout coverage, semantic freshness, heading hierarchy, citation quality, author expertise, schema presence.

Detects 40+ AI crawlers (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity and more) and flags whether they can reach your page — with fixes.
