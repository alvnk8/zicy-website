# Zicy — Module-to-ICP Value Matrix (Compiled)

**Purpose:** Map every Zicy module to the value it delivers each of the four ICPs — to feed the site-rework landing pages, the PM backlog, and GTM messaging.
**ICPs:** (1) Brands / branding professionals · (2) PR agencies / professionals · (3) Marketing / creative / media agencies · (4) Publishers / media companies.

---

## The platform spine (use on every page)

Zicy is the **truth layer for AI visibility**, and it's a **closed loop**: most tools stop at *measure*; Zicy goes **measure → diagnose → act → prove**.

- **Measure** — Brand Intelligence, AI Visibility, Competitive AI Performance, Competitor Sentiment, Brand Sentiment, Key Topics, Citation Analysis.
- **Diagnose** — Site Audit (AI-readiness), Visibility Gaps.
- **Act** — Action Center: LLMs.txt Generator, Schema Generator, New Content Writing, Content Optimization, Pages Summary.
- **Prove** — Site Traffic (GA4 / GSC / Bing / AI bot logs) and the AI-Visibility-vs-Brand-Search correlation.

The competitors' weakness is the gap between insight and action; Zicy owns that gap. Across all engines (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Google AI) and **all languages and markets** — APAC-native, global.

## Per-ICP value synthesis

### ICP 1 — Brands / branding professionals

**USP:** *AI is your brand's new first impression. Is it the brand you built?*

**Hero modules and the value each carries:**
- **Brand Intelligence** — the perception-vs-reality alignment gap, hallucination/fact-check, differentiator-loss, and reputation flags. This is the brand-health metric for the AI era.
- **Brand Sentiment** — exactly which descriptors AI uses, positive and negative, with frequency — the narrative beneath the score.
- **Site Traffic + correlation** — proof the brand work connects to real branded demand (the CMO's outcome question).

**Landing-page proof block:**
- See how every major AI engine describes your brand — and the gap to your intended positioning.
- Catch misrepresentation and reputation risk before it becomes the AI consensus.
- Recover the differentiators AI dropped.
- Track it across every language and market.

**Loss hook:** *Your hard-won positioning is being rewritten by machines — and most of it isn't surviving the translation.*

---

### ICP 2 — PR agencies / professionals

**USP:** *Reputation is now decided inside AI answers. Measure it. Move it. Prove it.*

**Hero modules and the value each carries:**
- **Brand Sentiment + Competitor Sentiment Profiles** — sentiment with named driver narratives, for the client and rivals; the work order, not just the symptom.
- **Citation Analysis** — Narrative Control, Hijack Rate ("how often a competitor wins the click instead"), and source/URL tracing — who is shaping the story, and where.
- **Brand Intelligence** — hallucination and distortion detection for crisis early-warning.

**Landing-page proof block:**
- Track how AI describes your clients and the specific narratives driving sentiment.
- Trace the exact sources feeding AI's view — and where competitors are hijacking the narrative.
- Catch distortions and hallucinations before they spread.
- A defensible post-AVE metric: share-of-citation and AI sentiment over time.

**Loss hook:** *Your earned media is now read by the model, not the reader. If it's repeating the wrong story, your client's reputation is already moving without you.*

---

### ICP 3 — Marketing / creative / media agencies

**USP:** *AI visibility — audited, fixed, and proven. White-label.*

**Hero modules and the value each carries:**
- **AI Visibility + Brand Intelligence** — the instant audit that wins the pitch.
- **Key Topics (Battlegrounds / Blind Spots) + Visibility Gaps** — the audit's gaps become the retainer scope and the content roadmap.
- **Action Center (Content Writing / Optimization / Schema / LLMs.txt)** — the billable execution layer, built in.
- **Site Traffic + correlation** — the ROI proof that renews the retainer.

**Landing-page proof block:**
- Win the pitch with an instant, branded AI-visibility audit.
- Turn gaps straight into scoped content and technical work.
- Execute in-platform — AI-optimized content, schema, llms.txt — compliant with Google's latest spam policies.
- Prove the lift with real traffic and demand data, client after client.

**Loss hook (for the agency to use on its own clients):** *Your clients are losing buyers inside AI answers they can't even see. Be the agency that shows them — and fixes it.*

---

### ICP 4 — Publishers / media companies

**USP:** *If AI doesn't recognize your content, your audience won't either.*

**Hero modules and the value each carries:**
- **Citation Analysis** — owned/earned/competitor citation split and URL-level attribution: are you the cited source, or is someone else taking credit?
- **Brand Intelligence (entity graph) + Key Topics** — whether AI recognizes your titles/IP as distinct entities, and which content topics it associates with you vs absent (blind spots).
- **Site Audit + Site Traffic (AI bot logs)** — whether AI crawlers can even access and read your catalogue, and how they're hitting it.

**Landing-page proof block:**
- See whether AI recognizes, cites, and correctly attributes your titles and IP.
- Find where AI is crediting a competitor for your content.
- Confirm AI crawlers can actually reach and parse your catalogue (llms.txt, robots, bot logs).
- Coverage across every language and market — built for multilingual catalogues.

**Loss hook:** *AI is deciding whether your catalogue exists. Right now it's summarizing your rivals and forgetting your IP.*

## Cross-cutting differentiators (the moat themes — reinforce everywhere)

1. **Truth, not just mentions** — fact-check and hallucination detection; perception-vs-reality alignment. Competitors count mentions; Zicy verifies them.
2. **The closed loop** — measure → diagnose → act → prove, in one platform. Competitors hand you data; Zicy hands you the fix and the proof.
3. **Outcome, not vanity** — Site Traffic and the demand correlation tie AI visibility to real clicks, CTR, and branded search.
4. **All engines, all languages, all markets** — APAC-native and global; most competitors are US/UK-centric.
5. **Compliant by design** — content tooling follows current AEO/GEO best practice and Google's latest spam policies, so output won't trigger scaled-content penalties.
